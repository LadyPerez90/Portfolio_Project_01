Who is DRCSyntax?

US Navy Submarine Veteran back in the private sector to make my dreams come to fruition. I'm a Manager and Senior Content Director for Nemesis Esports.
I've been gaming since Tetris was available on PC. Gaming is an escape from the normal pressures and worries of life and I'm glad we can all embrace it together!
In 2003 I joined the Digital Reality Crew, a Quake 3 clan, which is where the DRC in my name comes from. It's a fond memory and why I've decided to hang onto it long after the clan has become inactive.
